### Variable assignement: 
#Variables take on the **data type** of the values being assigned to them.

`>>> var0 = 7` <br>
`>>> var1 = 5.2` <br>
`>>> var2 = "hello"` <br>
`>>> var3 = True` <br>


#### Integer Variable:  
`>>> print(var0)` <br> 
` 7 ` <br>
`>>> type(var0)` <br>
` <type 'int'>` <br>

#### Floating point Variable:  
`>>> print(var1)` <br>
` 5.2 ` <br>
`>>> type(var1)` <br>
`<type 'float'>` <br>

#### String Variable:  
`>>> print(var2)` <br>
`hello` <br>
`>>> type(var2)` <br>
`<type 'str'>` <br>

#### Boolean Variable:  
`>>> print(var3)` <br>
`True` <br>
`>>> type(var3)` <br>
`<type 'bool'>` <br>

`>>> print(var1, var2, var3 )` <br>
`(5.2, 'hello', True)` <br>
